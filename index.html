My American Dream

I believe the process of programming encapsulates my American Dream. My experiences and past each make up lines of code in the program, and through the combination of all those lines, my American Dream is created This project is a walkthrough of my American Journey, and how it shaped my American Dream.

Early Life

FUNCTION EarlyLife(character){

SET character.name TO "Ryne Wang"

SET character.birthDate TO "04/03/2007"

SET character.birthPlace TO "Beijing, China"

As the psuedocode would suggest, I started my life in Beijing, China. Ironically, my roots in china play a part in my American Dream. Growig up in china my education wasn't exactly conventional. I attended a British private school where I picked up an London accent and some British spelling practices. All of this code contributes an unique background and multicultural experience.

Immigration

FUNCTION Immigration(character){

SET character.immigrationCountry TO "America"

SET character.immigrationYear TO "2014"

character.setChallenges(["language", "Culture"])

character.school = "Canyon View Elementary"

character.add(American Dream)

}

I first came to America in 2014. This was the culmination of my father's American Journey and the start of my own He had worked hard so we can leave china and start a new life in the States. The immigration process wasn't easy. I spoke very little English and didn't know any of the culture here Nonetheless, I started to adapt to life here. I attended Canyon View Elementary school, where I was quickly Americanized, and along with it I started dreaming of my American Dream.

New Life in America

FUNCTION newLife(character){

SET character.middleSchool TO "Tarbut V'Torah"

character.addExperiences ("Jewish Studies", "Hebrew")

character.AmericanDream.includeValue ("Diversity")

SET>character.highschool TO "Sage Hill School

}

For middle school, my parents sent me to a private Jewish School. There we had to take Jewish Studies and Hebrew classes. we also had to join in prayer twice a week. I am not and never had been religous, so alot of that didn't resonate with me, however the multicultural experience stayed with me. by the end of middle school I have grown up in china, attended a britsh school, learned hewbrew and studied torah. I believe my values embodies the melting pot that America is, and I believe that multiculturalness is a part of the American Dream

The Present

FUNCTION EnglishIII(character){

character.read(["The Great Gatsby", "The Awakening", "Nickel Boys"])

englishIII.discuss()

character.reflect()

character.AmericanDream.addValues ("Freedoms", "Opporunity")

character.AmericanDream.realize()

}

Now in the present, I am a junior at Sage Hill. Our work this year centered around the American Experience and the American Dream Some works we read this year was The Great Gatsby, The Awakening, and Nickel Boys. Through our reading, discussion and analysis of those books I developed a better understanding of what the American Dream is to me. A common thread among all our reading was the pursuit of freedoms and greater opportunities. I have come to value those as apart of my American Dream.
